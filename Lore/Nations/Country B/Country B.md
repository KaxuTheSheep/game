# Country B

*China-analogue. Authoritarian-capitalist centralised state. The Federal States' most significant external actor.*

---

## Overview

While the western world fragmented, Country B consolidated. Where other states sold off their functions, Country B tightened its grip — using the chaos of [[The Catalyst (2039)]] era as proof that liberal democratic models were structurally fragile, and accelerating its own centralisation accordingly.

Country B is not communist in any meaningful present-day sense. It is authoritarian and deeply capitalist — state-directed market economy, private enterprise operating under strict party oversight, enormous manufacturing and export capacity. The average citizen is not free, but is materially provided for within the system's terms. Dissent is managed, not tolerated.

It is the dominant centrally-planned economic and military counterweight to the Federal States' fragmented corporate sprawl.

---

## Relationship with New Halcyon

Country B's primary presence in [[New Halcyon]] is the [[Continental Freight Authority]] — originally a state-backed port and logistics division, established during New Halcyon's founding period. Whether the CFA still answers to Country B's state or has genuinely become independent is unknown to all parties in New Halcyon, including possibly the CFA itself.

Beyond the CFA, Country B exerts influence through proxy conglomerate investments — state-adjacent firms with Country B capital behind them, operating inside New Halcyon's economy without obvious state fingerprints.
