# Country C

*Japan-analogue. Automation and AI-labour origin point. Quasi-neutral technology supplier.*

---

## Overview

Country C's 2100s identity was shaped almost entirely by demographic collapse — a shrinking, ageing population that forced aggressive early investment in automation and AI-labour systems to sustain economic function. By the present day it is one of the most automated societies on the planet, with AI and robotic labour handling functions that elsewhere require human workforces.

It is quasi-neutral in the broader geopolitical rivalry between the Federal States and Country B — it sells technology to whoever pays, including both sides. Nobody fully trusts it. Nobody can afford to cut it off.

---

## Role in New Halcyon

Country C is the likely origin point for much of [[New Halcyon]]'s automation infrastructure and AI-adjacent technology. Its hardware and labour systems are licensed into the city's conglomerates, giving it quiet economic leverage without any overt political presence.

The android and AI-labour aesthetic running through New Halcyon — Ghost in the Shell-influenced — traces culturally and technologically back here.
