# The Ruins Pockets

*What survived when the rest was built over. Not ruins exactly. Something else.*

---

## What They Are

The ruins pockets are scattered fragments of pre-Catalyst New York construction — buildings and blocks that survived the city's transformation without being demolished, flooded, or absorbed into new development. They exist at the outer margins of New Halcyon, in areas conglomerates have not found worth developing and have not bothered to clear.

They are not uniformly ruined. Some structures are in reasonable condition by outskirt standards. Others are partially collapsed, partially occupied, partially something else. What none of them have is any of the infrastructure that defines New Halcyon's built environment — no self-healing concrete, no integrated building management, no AI systems, no drone infrastructure. These buildings predate all of it. They survive through structural luck and the basic durability of pre-Catalyst construction materials, not through any active maintenance.

---

## How They Were Occupied

The occupation of the ruins pockets was not organised. It was cumulative — individuals and small groups arriving for their own reasons, finding space, and staying.

The people who live here are not a community in the ideological sense. They do not share a programme or a collective identity. They share a geography and the practical conditions that geography imposes. Some are here because they cannot afford anywhere else. Some are avoiding surface faction entanglements. Some are operating businesses that require distance from conglomerate oversight. Some have simply been here long enough that leaving would require a reason.

The result looks less like a planned settlement than like Mumbai's informal districts — dense, specific, shaped by individual decisions accumulating over time into something that functions despite having no one design it. Buildings have been modified according to immediate need. Improvised markets occupy former loading bays. Upper floors of partially collapsed buildings have been made habitable through practical engineering with whatever materials were available.

---

## Faction Presence

The ruins pockets are not ungoverned in the absolute sense. They are ungoverned by the tier structure that applies to the rest of New Halcyon.

Non-conglomerate factions have extended into these areas as the communities there have grown. [[Commonweal]] has presence in several pockets, consistent with their mutual aid orientation and their interest in communities that the city's normal power structure has abandoned. Other factions — including some that do not have documented surface presence — operate here.

This faction presence does not dominate the community character. The people living in the ruins pockets are individuals with individual motivations. Faction influence is something they navigate rather than something that defines them.

---

## Relationship to the Rest of New Halcyon

The ruins pockets are not sealed off. People move between them and surface districts regularly. Goods, information, and people flow in both directions. Some residents of the ruins pockets work in surface districts. Some surface district residents use the ruins pockets as supply sources for salvage, cheap goods, or services that are not available or not legal elsewhere.

The pockets are visible from surface districts that border them. The visual contrast — pre-Catalyst construction against New Halcyon's accumulated architectural layers — is one of the most legible geographic markers of the city's edge.
