# The Outskirts

*Where the city thins out. Two different kinds of edge.*

---

## Overview

The outskirts of New Halcyon are not a single zone. They are where conglomerate control degrades and eventually disappears — but what fills that absence differs depending on which part of the outskirts you are in.

Two distinct types of outskirt territory exist. They share the characteristic of being beyond the margin of conglomerate interest. Everything else about them is different.

---

## The Ruins Pockets

Scattered across the outer sections of the city, mostly on the periphery that conglomerates have not yet absorbed or seen reason to develop.

These are pockets of pre-Catalyst New York construction — buildings from before the city's transformation that survived in enough structural form to be occupied, even if not in any condition that would be considered habitable by standard New Halcyon measures. Most of the old New York fabric was either demolished, built over, or flooded during the founding decades. These pockets are what remains where none of those things happened.

They have no self-healing concrete, no integrated AI systems, no building management infrastructure. What they have is walls and roofs — the minimum necessary for occupation.

See [[The Ruins Pockets]].

---

## The Vantage Ghost District

A different kind of outskirt entirely. Not ruined, structurally speaking. A fully built residential estate constructed during New Halcyon's early period by [[Vantage Group]] as an experiment in self-sufficient housing development. The experiment failed for reasons that had nothing to do with the buildings themselves.

The buildings still stand. They still heal themselves. The district is largely empty — a ghost town with residual occupation by individuals who have no better option or specific reason to be there.

Vantage has not reclaimed it and cannot easily do so. The buildings were constructed to non-standard specifications that predate Vantage's adoption of the city's common architectural grammar, and the self-healing systems are incompatible with current management infrastructure in ways that make integration more trouble than the territory is worth — for now.

See [[The Vantage Ghost District]].

---

## What the Outskirts Share

Both types of outskirt territory sit outside meaningful Tier 1 and Tier 2 coverage. What governance exists is Tier 3 at best, and in some sections nothing at all. Non-conglomerate factions have presence in both areas — [[Commonweal]] in particular has extended into outskirt communities, consistent with their mutual aid orientation.

The outskirts are not sealed off from the rest of the city. People move between them and surface districts. Goods move in both directions. The outskirts exist in relation to the rest of New Halcyon, not in isolation from it.
