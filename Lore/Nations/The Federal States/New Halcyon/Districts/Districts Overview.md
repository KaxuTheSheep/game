# Districts — Overview

*New Halcyon's districts are defined by culture, use, and territory. No formal zoning exists or has ever existed.*

---

## Design Principle

Districts feel organic and irregular — like real slum growth, which follows economic and social gravity rather than planning logic. Each district has an underlying cause for its character (conglomerate turf, old infrastructure, faction control, cultural concentration) but that cause is felt rather than signposted.

District boundaries are real but informal. They are marked by faction presence, building quality, the livery on patrolling drones, and local knowledge — not by signs or official demarcation.

---

## Pending
