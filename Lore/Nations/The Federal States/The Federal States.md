# The Federal States

*Country A. The eastern rump of the old country. Home of [[New Halcyon]].*

---

## Overview

The Federal States is what remained of the original nation after [[The Catalyst (2039)]] and the subsequent split. An eastern coastal corridor — urban, dense, financially and industrially powerful, but structurally resource-poor. It did not choose corporate governance so much as sign itself into it incrementally, and has never meaningfully reversed course.

By the 2100s present day, the Federal States is a patchwork: fully privatised zones like New Halcyon sitting alongside nominally governed but underfunded regions and ungoverned transitional areas in between. There is no meaningful central authority. The name persists on [[The Reserve]]'s currency and in diplomatic correspondence with nations that still bother.

---

## Geography

Eastern seaboard corridor. Urban concentration along the coast; interior regions are sparse, poorly serviced, and largely ignored by the conglomerates whose attention stays where the money is. Poor domestic food production — the agricultural heartland was lost to [[The United Territories]] in the split, a consequence nobody adequately planned for.

---

## Economy

- Financially and industrially dominant globally, despite internal inequality
- Structurally dependent on [[The United Territories]] for micronutrient food imports
- [[Synthetic Food]] is the primary caloric consumption method; keeps the population fed but not nourished
- [[The Reserve]] issues the currency; credibility is eroding
- No central economic policy — conglomerates self-regulate in practice

---

## Relationship with The United Territories

The defining background tension of the present day. The Federal States cannot easily move against the United Territories without triggering a food supply crisis; the United Territories cannot easily defend itself if the Federal States' conglomerates decide the dependency is worth breaking by force. Neither side has blinked yet. See [[Global Power Balance]].

---

## Cities

- **[[New Halcyon]]** — the flagship privatised city; the most (in)famous export of the Federal States model
- Cities 1 through X — earlier attempts at full privatisation, most of which failed during the 2040s–2060s founding period; some became ungoverned ruins, some were absorbed into functioning conglomerate territories, a few persist in degraded form
