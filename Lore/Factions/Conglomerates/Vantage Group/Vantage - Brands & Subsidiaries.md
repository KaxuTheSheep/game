# Vantage Group — Brands & Subsidiaries

*The parent name appears on almost nothing a resident encounters. The subsidiary names are everywhere.*

---

## Structure

Vantage operates the same brand invisibility model as Clarent — the parent company is technically public record and practically unknown at street level. Residents interact with subsidiary brands that feel like independent companies and have no reason to connect them to a single parent.

Unlike Clarent whose brand structure emerged partly from acquisitions with pre-existing identities, several of Vantage's subsidiary brands were created wholesale to serve specific market segments with specific cultural coding.

---

## Primary Subsidiaries

**Hearth Property Group**
Vantage's main residential management brand at the standard tier. The name is deliberately domestic and warm — a significant contrast to Vantage's own cold corporate neutrality. Hearth manages the day-to-day relationship with standard residential tenants: rent collection, maintenance requests, building communications. The warmth is performed and the performance is effective. Most standard tier residents who have a positive view of their building associate it with Hearth rather than knowing Hearth answers to Vantage.

**Meridian Residential** 
Premium tier residential management. Cleaner aesthetic than Hearth, oriented toward residents who want to feel they are making a sophisticated choice rather than simply being housed. Meridian's communications emphasise building quality, location, and amenity access. The Vantage parent name does not appear in Meridian materials.

**Cornerstone Commercial**
Manages Vantage's retail and commercial space portfolio — the commissaries, retail units, and commercial floors inside Vantage buildings. Cornerstone is the entity that negotiates with Clarent's franchise division over commissary access and terms. It presents as an independent commercial property management company. Clarent's people know it answers to Vantage. Most smaller commercial tenants do not.

**Lattice Data Services**
Vantage's data subsidiary. Officially a building analytics and smart infrastructure company providing services to property managers. Actually the entity through which Vantage monetises the data flowing through their building systems. Lattice sells aggregated and anonymised building data to research firms, commercial intelligence operations, and parties whose identity Lattice does not disclose publicly. The anonymisation is real in a technical sense and insufficient in a practical one — population level data at the granularity Lattice produces can be de-anonymised by anyone with sufficient additional data to cross-reference.

## Absorbed Ardent Subsidiaries

Several of Vantage's current subsidiaries were originally Ardent Systems properties absorbed during the post-War settlement. These retain their original branding in some cases because the brand equity was valuable and renaming them would have raised questions about the ownership change.

The specific subsidiaries absorbed from Ardent are pending detailed design but likely include at least one building security company that Vantage now operates as part of their enforcement structure — which is part of why Ardent's relationship with Vantage around security hardware is complicated by history as well as commercial interest.
