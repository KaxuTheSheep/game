# Security Forces Overview

*All armed organisations operating in New Halcyon above street-gang level.*

---

## Tier 1 — Conglomerate Forces

See [[Tier 1 Overview]].

Private armies. One per major conglomerate plus the [[Continental Freight Authority]] as a non-aligned entity. Protect directly-owned corporate property. Highest capability and professionalism. Funded directly by their parent conglomerate.

Security forces protecting civilian-facing conglomerate property (premium housing, commercial districts) are a subdivision of the conglomerate army — not a separate institution.

### Past Conflict

[[The War]] — an escalating inter-conglomerate conflict during New Halcyon's founding period — defines the present-day relationships between all Tier 1 forces. Turf boundaries, standing agreements, and deep institutional grudges all trace back to it.

## Tier 2 — Contracted Protection Firms

See [[Tier 2 Overview]].

Residential policing by subscription. Funded via rent line-items. Multiple competing firms. Coverage quality reflects payment tier.
