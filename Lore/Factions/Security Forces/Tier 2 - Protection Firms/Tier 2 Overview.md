# Tier 2 — Contracted Protection Firms

*The closest thing New Halcyon has to police. Funded by rent. Coverage varies.*

---

## Overview

Tier 2 firms are private protection companies that contract with residential landlords to provide security coverage for apartment blocks and residential areas. The cost is baked into tenant rent as a line-item — residents pay for policing as part of their housing cost, with no meaningful choice in provider (the landlord chooses, not the tenant).

Coverage quality scales directly with what the landlord paid for. A landlord who skimped on the protection contract gets minimal coverage. A landlord who invested gets visible patrols, faster response times, and better-equipped personnel.

---

## Visible Texture

Tier 2 firms are the most visually varied faction in New Halcyon. Each firm has its own uniform, colour scheme, drone livery, and vehicle markings. Walking through residential New Halcyon means passing through overlapping coverage zones — one firm's colours on one block, another's on the next, nobody's in between.

Turf changeover is visible and ordinary. Residents learn to read the markings the way people in the present day read street signs.

---

## What They Don't Cover

Commercial and business districts are covered by [[Tier 1 - Conglomerate Forces|Tier 1]] directly. Forgotten districts with no landlord investment and no contract have no Tier 2 presence — those areas fall to [[Street Level Overview|Tier 3]] by default.
