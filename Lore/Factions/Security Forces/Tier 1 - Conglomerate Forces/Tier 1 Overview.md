# Tier 1 — Conglomerate Forces

*Private armies. The highest tier of organised force in New Halcyon.*

---

## Overview

Each major conglomerate maintains a private army — funded directly, answering only to its parent corporation. These forces protect conglomerate-owned property (headquarters, premium residential, commercial districts, key infrastructure) and project conglomerate power in territorial disputes.

They are professional, equipped, and PR-conscious. A tier-1 incident that generates bad press is a problem for the conglomerate's brand, which creates a layer of restraint that lower-tier forces lack. They are not police and do not operate as police in non-conglomerate territory — that is a deliberate distinction maintained for legal and reputational reasons that no longer have any legal backing, but persist as institutional habit.

---

## The Army / Security Subdivision Relationship

Each conglomerate army contains a security subdivision responsible for civilian-facing protection: premium residential buildings, commercial districts, executive zones. This subdivision is part of the army's operational scope — not a separate company or institution. It behaves more visibly and more politely than the army's other functions; it is still the army.

---

## The Continental Freight Authority

[[Continental Freight Authority|The CFA]] operates at this tier but is not affiliated with any New Halcyon conglomerate. It is the former port and logistics division of [[Country B]], established in New Halcyon during the founding period. Its current loyalty — to Country B's state, or genuinely independent — is unknown. Every other Tier 1 force treats it with careful neutrality for this reason.

---

## The War

See [[The War]]. The foundational event of all Tier 1 relationships. An escalating series of inter-conglomerate disputes that nearly destroyed New Halcyon before it had stabilised. The settlement that ended it created the present-day turf arrangements. The grudges it created have not fully resolved.
