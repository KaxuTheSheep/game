# Street Level — Overview

*What fills the gap where no contract reaches.*

---

## The Gap

[[Tier 2 - Protection Firms|Tier 2]] coverage requires a landlord willing to pay for it. In New Halcyon's forgotten districts — areas too poor, too peripheral, or too chaotic to attract conglomerate investment — no contract exists. No firm patrols. No drone carries a corporate livery. What exists instead is whatever the residents themselves have built, or whatever predatory structure has moved in to exploit the absence.

---

## Militias

Three distinct militias operate across New Halcyon's ungoverned residential areas. Each has a different origin and character; they are not interchangeable.

- [[Ninth Watch]] — grew from neighbourhood watch culture; community-rooted, pragmatic
- [[Greyback Company]] — ex-Tier-1 veterans who went independent after [[The War]]; professional but detached from corporate loyalty
- [[Commonweal]] — ideologically-driven mutual aid; the only faction at any tier that operates from genuine political conviction rather than profit or survival

In areas where a militia operates, residents generally receive more consistent coverage than in gang-controlled areas — not because militias are necessarily better armed, but because their incentive structure is more aligned with the community they're in.

---

## Gangs

Multiple gangs operate across New Halcyon, each controlling specific turf. They extract protection fees from residents — inconsistently, often predatorially. The coverage they provide in exchange is real but unreliable: a gang that is present and attentive one week may be distracted by a turf dispute the next, leaving their "protected" residents exposed.

Gang naming convention: named after streets, defunct pre-collapse businesses, or squatted old corporate branding within their territory. See [[Gangs Convention & Index]].

In heavily gang-afflicted areas, residents deal with the gang whether they choose to or not. In less afflicted areas, a militia may operate instead — the boundary between these conditions is fluid and can shift with a single turf change.
