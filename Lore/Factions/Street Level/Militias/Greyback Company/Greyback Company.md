# Greyback Company

*Ex-Tier-1 veterans. Professional detachment without corporate loyalty.*

---

## Origin

Greyback Company was founded by former Tier 1 conglomerate soldiers in the aftermath of [[The War]] — people who had fought in the inter-conglomerate conflict, survived it, and walked away from corporate employment either by choice or because the restructuring that followed left no place for them.

The founder — referred to as Greyback, whether a callsign, a surname, or a nickname is unclear — is a pinned character for future development. They represent the specific disillusionment of someone who was good at corporate violence and decided to stop doing it for corporations.

---

## Character

Professional and disciplined in a way the other militias are not — these are trained soldiers, not community volunteers or idealists. That professionalism is both an asset (effective, organised, reliable) and a distance: Greyback Company is not embedded in the community it protects the way [[Ninth Watch]] is. It operates more like a contractor than a neighbour.

This creates an interesting tension: the most capable of the three militias is also the least organically connected to the people it serves. Its loyalty is to its own code rather than to any community or ideology.
