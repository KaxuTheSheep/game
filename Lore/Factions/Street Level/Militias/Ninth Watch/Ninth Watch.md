# Ninth Watch

*Block-watch origin. The most community-rooted of the three militias.*

---

## Origin

The Ninth Watch grew from literal neighbourhood watch culture — residents of a specific district organising to fill the security gap left by absent Tier 2 coverage. It was not founded as a militia; it became one incrementally as the gap proved permanent and the threat level required more than unarmed observation.

The "Ninth" in the name refers to its district of origin — pending district design.

---

## Character

Pragmatic and community-embedded. The Ninth Watch is not ideological; it does not have a political programme beyond the immediate welfare of the people in its coverage area. Its members are residents first and security personnel second. It operates on local knowledge and local relationships in a way that neither Tier 1 nor Tier 2 forces can replicate.

This makes it more trusted in its territory than any contracted firm would be. It also makes it less portable — the Ninth Watch's effectiveness is inseparable from its specific geographic and social context.
