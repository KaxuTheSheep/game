# Commonweal

*The only faction in New Halcyon that means what it says.*

---

## Origin

Commonweal was founded as an explicitly ideological project — mutual aid and community defence as a political act, not just a practical response to absent policing. Where [[Ninth Watch]] is pragmatic and [[Greyback Company]] is professional, Commonweal is principled. It believes something. In a city where every other power structure is motivated by profit, survival, or institutional inertia, that makes it genuinely unusual.

---

## Character

Warm-named deliberately — "Commonweal" gestures toward an older language of shared civic life that New Halcyon has never had and Commonweal is attempting to build from the ground up. Whether that project is naive, admirable, or both is an open question the setting should leave genuinely unresolved.

It operates on mutual aid principles: resources shared within the community, decisions made collectively, protection provided not as a transaction but as a social contract. This is its strength (deep community trust, genuine solidarity) and its structural vulnerability (harder to scale, slower to decide, dependent on ideological commitment that can fracture under pressure).

---

## Position in the Setting

Commonweal is the setting's most direct challenge to the dominant logic of New Halcyon — the proof-of-concept that the city's operating assumptions are not inevitable. The conglomerates are aware of it. Some find it a useful PR counterpoint ("see, people have choices"). Others find it a quiet threat. None have moved against it directly yet.
