# Gangs — Convention & Index

*Multiple gangs. Named by territory and history. Documented here as encountered.*

---

## Naming Convention

Gangs in New Halcyon are named after the specific geography and history of their turf rather than invented names. Three primary sources:

**Streets and locations** — named after the street, block, or landmark they control. Simple, direct, legible. The name tells you where they are.

**Defunct pre-collapse businesses** — named after a company or brand that used to occupy their territory and no longer exists. Common in areas that were commercial before the corporate consolidation left them forgotten. Carries an ironic quality — a gang named after a dead fast food chain, a closed bank, a demolished mall.

**Squatted corporate branding** — named after a conglomerate or subsidiary that *does* still exist but has abandoned the area, or whose signage/infrastructure remains even as the company moved on. More aggressive irony — using the name of a company that left you behind as your identity.

---

## Design Note

Gangs should feel numerous and specific rather than generic. The naming convention is the generator — new gangs can be created as needed using it, tied to the district design they appear in. They are not pre-designed as a full roster; they emerge from the map.
