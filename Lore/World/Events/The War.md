# The War

The inter-conglomerate conflict that nearly destroyed New Halcyon before it had properly established itself. Not planned, not declared — accumulated.

---

## What It Was

A series of small, individually unremarkable disputes between [[Tier 1 - Conglomerate Forces|tier-1 conglomerate security forces]], each begun on its own terms over its own ostensible cause, that escalated progressively because no state mechanism existed to de-escalate them. Nobody started a war. Everyone started something smaller. The war is what those smaller things became.

---

## When

During the 2040s–2060s founding period of New Halcyon — after the conglomerates had established real power and territory, but before the city had stabilised into its present-day arrangement.

---

## Why It Matters Now

- Present-day turf boundaries, alliances, and grudges in New Halcyon trace directly back to wartime settlements
- [[Greyback Company]] — the ex-tier-1 veteran militia — is a direct product of the war; its founder (pinned character, TBD) walked away from corporate soldiering in its aftermath
- The war is part of why most of Cities 1 through X failed; New Halcyon's survival of it is part of what makes the city notable
- The [[Continental Freight Authority]] established itself in New Halcyon partly during the chaos of the war's later stages — its exact entry conditions are deliberately ambiguous
